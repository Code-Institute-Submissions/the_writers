The writers monthly cash give away

Every month on the writers we hold a competition for our subscribers in which they can win a cash prize. The more subscribers we have, the bigger the prize for the winner.

There are two stages to each competition. The entry period and the voting period. Once the voting period ends, the entry with the most votes wins the cash

  • The entry period

    • Subscribers can submit their work to the competition during this time.

  • The voting period

    • Once the entry period ends, the voting period begins. No submissions or edits can be made during this time. Users can vote for the entry they think deserves to win the cash prize.

  • The Prize

    • The reason we ask competition entries to come from subscribers only is that we use the subscription money to fun the winners pot. This means the more subscribers we have, the bigger the prize will be
