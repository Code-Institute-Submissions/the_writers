About

Introducing The Writers

The Writers is a space for people who like to share what they write with the world. Writers can discuss what they have written with their readers gaining valuable insight on their work.

Guidelines

The Do's and Don'ts

  1. Hate speech, or profanity directed toward other users is prohibited;

    • If you would like to report an offensive post please Email TheWriters86@gmail.com with a link to the post.

  2. Follow the brief;

    • Competition entries not following the brief will be disqualified and you will not be eligible for a refund on your subscription.

  3. Share your posts;

    • Sharing your posts on social media is a great way for more people to see your work.

  4. Make comments;

    • Help others by offering constructive criticism. Tell them why you like or don't like something about their work.

  5. Vote;

    • Make sure you vote for the entries you think deserve to win. If you have entered a competition yourself, use social media to get yourself more votes!
